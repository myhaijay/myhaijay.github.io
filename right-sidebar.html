House Price Prediction

In the Real Estate Industry, profit maximization and business sustenance is achieved by the ability to sell house properties. Consequently, understanding the factors and how these factors influence house prices, becomes of utmost importance. Using King County, Washington in the US as a case study, I used the dataset of properties (Houses) that were sold between 2014 and 2015. This dataset has over 20,000 housing units with over 19 unique features describing them. To present a Machine Learning model that is based on a Regression Algorithm, I worked with MySQL and Tableau to understand the dataset before I proceeded to Python to build the model.
